This contains 20th-century reanalysis v3 datasets in netcdf format.

Large_Domain_Netcdf: Is on a domain of latitude 35–70◦ N and longitude 45◦ W–30◦ E.
Small_Domain_Netcdf: Is on a domain of latitude 45–60◦ N and longitude 20◦ W–05◦ E.
 
20th-century reanalysis v3, Ensemble Mean is gathered at the hour 15:00 from the website https://psl.noaa.gov/data/gridded/data.20thC_ReanV3.html, and last accessed on 21/01/2022.
